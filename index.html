Robert P. Levy, AKA rplevy

Professional software engineer & amateur cybernetician living in San Francisco. BA Cognitive Science & Lingustics, SUNY Oswego 2000. Clojure specialist since 2009. Interests include applying embodied cognitive science to problems of meaning in software, situated programming, intelligence amplification, agent-centered software architectures, event-based stream processing and dataflow programming, and decentralized & autonomous organization. Currently working at TalkIQ, doing frontend development in ClojureScript, developing a product for augmenting human sales and support agents and organizations by means of real-time and post-call analysis and decision support based on voice data.
